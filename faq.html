Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SmartAgri?

SmartAgri is a youth-driven agricultural platform that promotes modern farming using technology, innovation, and training.

2. Who can join SmartAgri programs?

Anyone passionate about farming, especially youth and smallholder farmers, can join and benefit from our initiatives.

3. Do you offer physical training or online only?

We provide both physical and online training to ensure all farmers can access knowledge regardless of location.

4. How can I get farming tools from SmartAgri?

We connect farmers with trusted suppliers and sometimes offer subsidized tools through our programs.

5. How can I contact SmartAgri support?

You can use the contact form or reach us via phone/email listed on the Contact page.
